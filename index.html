THE MAKER

One Engineer.
Every Product.

Every product sold under the Waypoint name is designed, programmed, machined, finished, and shipped by the same person — a mechanical and manufacturing engineer with roughly a decade of experience across aerospace, automotive, plastics, consumer electronics, and food and beverage industries.

No design team, no outsourced production, no dropshipping. A real person making real things in a real shop.
